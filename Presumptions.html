Presumptions
ONE WAS THAT THE GRENADE was a device of decisive devastation.  A little bomb.  I thought you pulled out the grenade to make a big hit, and when I read about real war taking place with guns I wondered why, when good guys were losing, they didn't just pull out the grenades and get it over with . . . alternately entertained that there was a code, as of fair play or politeness, that prohibited the use of this magnificent device among fighting gentlemen.  But grenades aren't for blowing the shit out of people.  Not where I now come from.  The grenade is an asked question, a shocker before the shot,  a means of scaring up snakes.
