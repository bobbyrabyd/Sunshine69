Specter

UP ASHBY AVE. TWELVE HOURS LATER an apparition in glowing lime green was seen to stroll ethereally eight inches above the sidewalk, jerking on brittle tree branches and assimilating some of the succulent trouble of Berkeley bordering Oakland.  It was my land.  I made the most of it in final ectoplasmic moments.
