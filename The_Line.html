The Line

WHAT'S WRONG WITH YOU? Can't you see I'm tall, dark, and handsome?

That was one of the lines that made the white ladies look twice. It was a funny fix to get them thinking I fit the stereotype handed them of the ideal male. Oh, is that what it means? Sure, why not! It got Carol to give me a second look, and Patty laughed right out because she has a good sense of humor about the race thing and I could tell she'd be easy to deal with. No deception necessary.
