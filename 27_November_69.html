27 November 69

WHEN MICK SAYS We're gonna do one more and then we're gonna go he's thinking about backstage--getting whisked to club or hotel and some fair chi-keeta he's brought with him or a well-trained road he's selected.

This is the utterance that will get etched for immortality on a million-odd faces of acetate.  This is the concert that is recorded for the fans who haven't been born.

When we did the shoot for this we had a laugh.  Trussed up in Old Glory, Charlie paraded around on the back of an ass.  The Maysles were there as always rolling.  Too bad Brian couldn't make it.  He would have enjoyed himself.

When Mick talks about a poor boy he's talking about him.

When Mick says the time is right for violent revolution he's right.
