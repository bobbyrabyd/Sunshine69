Satin

Satin as pseudonym for Franky Avylon and his breed. Avylon, the clean-cut, satin-sporting crooner, was at the top of the charts with his syrupy pop during the Stones' first crusade through America. Ragtag rockers like the Stones preached long and hard before swaying the flock from this false prophet.
