Birds 1
 BLACKBIRDS WILL SWOOP AND SWARM before astorm, outdo each other making harrowing twirls and taking perilousdives.  Then the whole flock will break into a mass dash and be gone. Blackbirds act so mad sometimes, in their chaotic numbers, even the  crows retreat a safe distance,either sitting back in a tree or slowly swooping overhead until it coolsdown.
